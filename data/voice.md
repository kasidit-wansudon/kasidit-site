# Kasidit site voice

Operational, not poetic. Every claim either cites a number or names a mechanism.

## Do
- Short sentences, present tense, plain English.
- Cite the source of every number (e.g. "SWE-bench Lite strict", "7-task curated Opus run").
- Prefer mechanism over metaphor: "the framework pulls it back" beats "it stays aligned".
- Use the working vocabulary: discipline, narrow, delegate, synthesize, runtime is the judge, checklists, gravity, tier.

## Don't
- No adjectives that carry no information (powerful, robust, seamless, elegant).
- No marketing verbs: empowers, unleashes, transforms, revolutionizes.
- No second-person pep talk ("you'll love", "imagine if").
- No exclamation marks. No emojis. No hashtags.
- No claims the benchmark table does not support.
